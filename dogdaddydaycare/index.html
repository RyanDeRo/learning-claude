About Dog Daddy Daycare

Welcome! At Dog Daddy Daycare, your furry friend isn't just a guest—they're part of the family. Located in Philadelphia, we offer premium in-home dog boarding and daycare services where your pup gets to enjoy all the comforts of a real home.

Your dog will have access to a spacious, fully fenced yard for safe outdoor play, and we're conveniently located near a beautiful park for daily walks and adventures. Unlike traditional kennels, your dog will sleep comfortably inside the home and receive personalized attention throughout their stay.

Plus, they'll have the chance to socialize with our friendly resident dog, Vito, and even meet our curious cat, Olive!

Services We Offer

🏠 In-Home Dog Boarding

Your dog stays at our home with full access to comfortable indoor spaces. They'll sleep inside, relax on the couch, and be treated like family—not kept in a kennel or cage.

🌞 Doggy Daycare

Drop your pup off for the day while you're at work or running errands. They'll enjoy playtime in our fenced yard, walks to the park, and socialization with Vito.

🎾 Daily Exercise & Play

Multiple play sessions throughout the day in our secure, fenced yard plus walks to the nearby park for fresh air and exploration.

❤️ Personalized Care

We accommodate special needs, dietary requirements, medication schedules, and any other specific care instructions for your dog.
