What is CharitySpar?

CharitySpar is a mobile app that empowers jiu-jitsu athletes to turn their passion into purpose. Log your training sessions, set fundraising goals, and watch as your supporters pledge donations based on your rolls. Whether you're training for competition or just staying active, every session can make a real-world impact.

How It Works

Getting started is simple. In just a few steps, you can start turning your training into charitable impact.

1

Create Your Campaign

Choose a charity and set your fundraising goal. Create a compelling story about why you're rolling for this cause.

2

Get Pledges

Share your campaign with friends, family, and the BJJ community. Supporters pledge per roll or flat donations.

3

Train & Log

Hit the mats and log your training sessions in the app. Watch your impact grow with every roll.

4

Make an Impact

Funds are automatically sent to your chosen charity. Share updates and celebrate your success!

Why CharitySpar?

The jiu-jitsu community is built on principles of respect, discipline, and mutual growth. CharitySpar extends these values beyond the gym, giving athletes a platform to leverage their training for good. Whether you're raising funds for environmental conservation, supporting underprivileged youth programs, or funding medical research, CharitySpar makes it easy to combine your passion for BJJ with meaningful charitable action.
