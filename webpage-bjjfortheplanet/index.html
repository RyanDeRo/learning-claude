Our Mission

BJJ For The Planet combines the discipline and community of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with meaningful charitable and social impact. We've deployed an app, charityspar, support events and Jiu-Jitsu travel, and partner with organizations like Guardian Gyms and Jungle Keepers to spread charity and protect our planet's most vulnerable ecosystems.

Through charity, technology, and travel, we bring together martial artists from around the world to make a real difference—one roll at a time.
