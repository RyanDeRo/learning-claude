Mancora, Peru Charity Retreat

March 2026

Our inaugural charity retreat brought together jiu-jitsu practitioners from around the world to the beautiful coastal town of Mancora, Peru. In partnership with Guardian Gyms, we combined intensive BJJ training with meaningful conservation work in the Amazon rainforest region and local community support initiatives.

Training Highlights

  • Daily BJJ classes with black belt instructors
  • Open mat sessions and technique workshops
  • Beach training and conditioning
  • Competition preparation seminars
  • Cultural exchange with local martial artists

Conservation & Community Work

  • Rainforest reforestation project
  • Beach and coastal cleanup initiatives
  • English language classes for local youth
  • School supply and equipment donations
  • Partnership with local conservation NGOs
"This retreat changed my perspective on what it means to be a martial artist. Training alongside people from different countries while working together to plant trees and help the local community was an experience I'll never forget. BJJ For The Planet showed me that we can use our passion for jiu-jitsu to make a real difference in the world."
— Sarah Martinez, Purple Belt, California
"The combination of world-class training and meaningful conservation work made this more than just a vacation—it was a mission. Seeing the direct impact we had on the rainforest and local schools reminded me why I love this community. I can't wait for the next event!"
— Marcus Johnson, Brown Belt, New York
